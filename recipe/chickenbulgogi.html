Chicken Bulgogi Rice

Description

Serves 3-4

Simple Chicken Bulgogi Rice dish, good for quick weeknight dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 green onions
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1.3 lb boneless chicken thigh
  • 1-2 Vietnamese dried chilies
  • 1 tbsp neutral-tasting oil
  • 1 tsp roasted sesame oil
  • 3-4 cups cooked rice
  • generous pinch of toasted sesame seeds, to garnish

Bulgogi Sauce

  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp light corn syrup
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1/2 tsp dark soy sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
  • black pepper, to taste

Steps

Prep

  1. Thinly slice green onions, seperate greens and whites. Chop garlic
  2. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Remove skin (optional).
  3. In a small bowl, add 3 cloves minced garlic along with the rest of the Bulgogi sauce ingredients. Mix well.
  4. Add torn pieces of dried chilies into the bowl.

Cooking

  1. In large pan, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken after it gets hot. Cook 6-7 minutes.
  2. Add whites of green onions. Saute until fragrant.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and pour in sauce. Stir for 1-2 minutes or until sauce thickens.
  4. Turn heat off. Add sesame oil.

Assembly

  1. Serve over rice, adding greens of green onions and sesame seeds to garnish.
